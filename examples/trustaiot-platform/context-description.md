# Context Description — TrustAIoT Platform

## System overview
- System: Trustworthy AIoT Platform
- Purpose: Host and govern multiple AIoT projects under trustworthiness constraints
- Architecture: Edge–fog–cloud, heterogeneous devices
- Lifetime: Long-term, evolving platform

## Organisational context
- Platform owner organisation
- Multiple internal platform teams
- External project teams and partners
- Regulatory and compliance bodies

## Legal and regulatory context
- Applicable regulation: GDPR, AI Act, sector-specific IoT regulation
- Jurisdiction: EU
- Constraints: Device lifecycle management, data protection, accountability

## Social and ethical context
- Impact on users and communities
- Environmental sustainability
- Inclusion and accessibility
- Risk of opaque decision-making in embedded AI

## Trustworthiness priorities

| Dimension | Priority | Rationale |
|----------|----------|-----------|
| Lawfulness | High | Regulatory exposure |
| Robustness | High | CPS and IoT risks |
| Explicability | High | Human-centric trust |
| Sustainability | Medium | Environmental impact |
| Fairness | Medium | Diverse user contexts |

## Revision triggers
- Regulatory updates
- New device classes
- Platform incidents
- Expansion to new domains
